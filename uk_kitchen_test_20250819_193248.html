Q: How do I maintain the sharpener over time? A: Use a soft cloth for cleaning and avoid immersing in water. The discs won't wear quickly under normal home use, so maintenance is minimal and you'll save time compared with sending knives out for sharpening.

Q: How does Sheffield Master compare to electric sharpeners? A: Electric models can remove more steel hastily, shortening knife life. Sheffield Master's diamond and ceramic discs work precisely and preserve blade integrity, a better long-term investment.
